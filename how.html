How It Works

From story — to action.
Memory becomes life.

Any community, school, or organization can lay a cornerstone of social repair and Jewish solidarity — through a single memorial initiative they choose, learn, adopt, and build together.

The Path in Four Steps

How It Works

Step 01
Choose
The community chooses, from among the memorial initiatives, a project close to its heart.
Step 02
Learn
They get to know the person, their story, and the values of heroism and resilience they embodied.
Step 03
Adopt
They become active partners in the commemoration — in the local community or in Israel.
Step 04
Build
An educational, financial, and entrepreneurial partnership — and the path is only beginning.

The Role of the Connecting Side

What We Actually Do

A precise match

We match each community with a memorial initiative close to its heart, and connect it to the bereaved family behind it.

Guidance along the way

We guide the community from the first meeting to an established partnership — tailoring the process to its needs.

Educational materials

We provide the community with learning and activity materials about the person and their values, and together build a meaningful learning process within the community.

Subsidy and fundraising

We subsidize the community's visit to the initiative and work to raise resources to expand the activity to many communities.

A Story of Continuity

From story — to action. Memory becomes life.

01 · The Story
In a classroom, across the sea
Bereaved parents tell students about their fallen son — about his values, his heroism, and his giving.
And from there —
to Israel
02 · The Action
On the ground, in Israel
Students and volunteers head out into the field — taking an active part in expanding the memorial initiative and in building the land.

The Initiative's Outcomes

What remains after — and what keeps growing

Widening the circle of memory for the nation's heroes
Strengthening Jewish resilience and identity against the challenges of the times
Creating a real path for social action in Israel
Educating toward initiative, taking responsibility, and active Jewish involvement
Forging new paths between Israelis and their brethren in the Diaspora

Choose the level of involvement that suits your community, or talk to us and we'll build the path together.
