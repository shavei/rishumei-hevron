Out of the Rupture — the Choice to Grow

Many bereaved families chose to remember and create new life.

The families of these heroes founded dozens of initiatives of memory and life in honor of their loved ones — many of them meant to help Israeli society grow and the nation rise again, born of the most terrible price of all.
