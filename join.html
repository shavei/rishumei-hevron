Who Takes Part — and How to Join the Path

The initiative connects the memorial initiatives in Israel with Jewish communities and organizations around the world. Any community, school, or organization can lay a cornerstone of social repair, Jewish solidarity, and an education toward a life of meaning and giving.

Who the Initiative Is For

Two Sides We Connect

The Giving Side
Memorial initiatives in Israel
Initiatives founded by bereaved families in memory of a fallen loved one — active or in their final stages of execution, with an emphasis on those that help society and Israel grow.
The Partner Side
Jewish communities and organizations
Jewish schools Central synagogues Jewish federations Youth organizations Charity and welfare organizations

In the next stage of the initiative, a track for individuals will be built, allowing them too to take part in the commemoration and the partnership.

The Partnership Model

Three Levels of Involvement

Every initiative chosen by a local Jewish body is supported through all stages of implementation — at the level of involvement that suits the community.

BASIC
Local Support
Financial support for all stages of local implementation: carrying out a local commemoration project, hosting a delegation from the bereaved family, and spreading the story of the fallen.
Talk to Us
PREMIUM
Partnership in Israel
+ Everything in Local Support
In addition — involvement in the commemoration project in Israel, financial partnership, and involvement in growing and spreading it.

Full Involvement
PLATINUM
Leadership and Expansion
+ Everything in Premium
In addition — delegations to Israel (subsidized by the nonprofit), significant promotion of the initiative, and replicating it in additional places.

The Bishvileinu nonprofit works to raise additional resources to expand the initiative to many communities and to subsidize the delegations coming to Israel.
