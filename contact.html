Everything you wanted to know

Jewish schools, central synagogues, Jewish federations, youth organizations, and charity and welfare organizations — as well as bereaved families who have founded a memorial initiative and wish to share it with communities around the world. In the next stage, a track for individuals will also be built.
We match each community with a memorial initiative close to its heart, and connect it to the bereaved family behind it. The community learns about the person and their values, and decides whether to act in its own local area or to be part of the building in Israel.
Local Support (BASIC) — financial support for the local implementation; Partnership in Israel (PREMIUM) — involvement in the commemoration project in Israel; and Leadership and Expansion (PLATINUM) — delegations to Israel, significant promotion, and replicating the initiative in additional places. See the full model →
We are the connecting side: we make the match between families and communities, guide the project, tailor each community's initiative to it, help with educational materials, and subsidize the community's visit to the initiative.
