About Us

The Bishvileinu initiative was created to build the bridge between memory in Israel and Jewish communities around the world.

The initiative's purpose is to strengthen the bond between Israel and Diaspora Jewry — by widening the circle of memory and growth of the fallen's memorial initiatives into Jewish communities around the world.

The Challenging Years of Diaspora Jewry

Between Historic Mutual Solidarity and a Global Wave of Antisemitism

Support
Mutual solidarity on a historic scale
In the face of the grievous blow and despite the risk, many across the communities chose to support their brothers and sisters in Israel openly — with donations and aid unprecedented in history.
The Challenge
A dramatic rise in antisemitism
From the war's earliest days, a global wave of antisemitism has eroded the communities' resilience. Jewish and Zionist education faces a challenge — and the need arises for new paths of resilience, identity, partnership, and hope.

Out of the Rupture — the Choice to Grow

Many bereaved families chose to remember and create new life.

The families of these heroes founded dozens of initiatives of memory and life in honor of their loved ones — many of them meant to help Israeli society grow and the nation rise again, born of the most terrible price of all.

How

A Three-Way Partnership

Three Sides, One Path

The Giving Side
The bereaved families
They reveal the memorial initiatives to communities, enabling individuals and groups to build — and be built — by expanding them.
The Connecting Side
The people of Bishvileinu
We make the match, guide along the way, tailor each community's initiative to it, and subsidize the encounter.
The Partner Side
Communities and organizations
They open a door, learn, adopt — and partner educationally, financially, and entrepreneurially in growing the project.

A double meaning to the name "Bishvileinu"

In Hebrew, the name "Bishvileinu" carries two meanings at once:

"For Us"

For us they fought and paid with their lives. For us, new initiatives were created in their memory, to make Israel a better place.

"On Our Path"

The journey along the path of their lives — tracing their values and acting through the initiative that commemorates them — is a journey on a new, shared path for the Diaspora communities and Israel; a path that strengthens partnership and resilience in an Israel rising from the rupture and in the challenges of the Jewish world.

Let us reach out, join, and widen the circle of action — for them and for us.
