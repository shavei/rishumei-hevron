Who we are

The story behind Bishvileinu

An initiative born of pain and of choice — to bridge remembrance in Israel with Jewish communities around the world.

The background

The heroism of the fallen united the nation

Solidarity — the whole nation

The nation came out in throngs to accompany the fallen on their final journey — a moment of deep national kinship. Their words of life became stickers and the voices of an entire generation.

The Diaspora — years of challenge

On the one hand, a dramatic rise in antisemitism that struck at the resilience of communities. On the other — mutual responsibility on a historic scale: open support for Israel through unprecedented donations and aid.

A dual meaning

One name, two meanings

Our path

The journey along the path of their lives, the search for their values, and active work in an initiative that honors them — is a journey along a new, shared path of Diaspora communities and Israel. A meaningful path that will strengthen partnership and resilience in an Israel growing out of the rupture, and amid the challenges of the Jewish world in the Diaspora.

For our sake

For our sake they fought and paid with their lives. For our sake new initiatives were created in their memory, to make Israel a better place.

Let us reach out, join in, and widen the circle of action — for them and for us.

The purpose of the initiative

Widening the circle of memory

"Bishvileinu" widens the circle of memory and growth into the Jewish communities of the world. Communities are introduced to these exemplary figures, adopt an initiative of living memory, and become active partners in the building of Israel.

It is a gateway to meaningful involvement in building and strengthening Israeli society — an entry point to initiatives of resilience, community, culture, welfare, and educational work.

A three-way partnership

Three sides, one path

The giving side

The bereaved families

They open their memorial initiatives to communities, allowing individuals and groups to build — and to be built — by expanding them.

The connecting side

The people of 'Bishvileinu'

They make the match, accompany the journey, tailor the right initiative to each community, and subsidize the encounter.

The partnering side

Communities and organizations

They open a door, learn, adopt — and partner educationally, financially, and entrepreneurially in growing the project.

The vision

The journey does not end at the encounter —
it is only the beginning,
and from there the sky is the limit.

For memory is the bridge between ruin and redemption.
