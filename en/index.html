Who we are

The bridge between memory and communities

The Bishvileinu initiative was created to build the bridge between memorial projects in Israel and Jewish communities around the world.

🔗

What we are

A connecting and guiding body

We match a memorial initiative with a community, and accompany the bond from the first meeting to a lasting partnership.

🛤️

How

Matching · Guidance · Subsidy

Precise connection, educational materials, and subsidy of the encounter — so the partnership begins and endures.

🌍

Why it matters

Shared Jewish resilience

We strengthen the identity and resilience of communities, and build stronger bridges between world Jewry and Israel.
