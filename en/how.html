The mechanism

How it works

From the first meeting to a lasting partnership — a step-by-step guide.

What we do

Four core services

🎯

Service 1

Precise matching

We match each community with a memorial initiative close to its heart, and connect it to the bereaved family behind it.

🤝

Service 2

Guidance along the way

We accompany the community from the first meeting to a lasting partnership — tailoring the process to its needs.

💰

Service 3

Subsidy and fundraising

We subsidize the community's visit to the initiative, and work to raise resources to expand the activity to many communities.

📚

Service 4

Educational materials

We adapt for each community the Zionist and educational values learned from the soldier's story, and connect students to meaningful action.

The path

Four steps, one path

Step 01

Choose

The community chooses, among the memorial initiatives, a project close to its heart.

Step 02

Learn

They get to know the person, their story and their values of heroism and resilience.

Step 03

Adopt

They become active partners in remembrance — in the local community or in Israel.

Step 04

Build

An educational, economic and entrepreneurial partnership — and the path is only beginning.

Outcomes of the initiative

What comes of it

✓

Widening the circle of remembrance of our nation's heroes

✓

Strengthening Jewish resilience and identity amid the challenges of our time

✓

Creating a real path for social action in Israel

✓

Educating toward initiative, taking responsibility and active Jewish involvement

✓

Creating new paths between Israelis and their brethren in the diaspora

Proof from the field

From a story — to action

Here's what a real partnership looks like in practice.

01

In the classroom, across the sea

Bereaved parents tell students in the community about their son — his life, his values and his heroism. An encounter that connects heart to heart, across the ocean.

02

On the ground, in Israel

The partners from the diaspora come to Israel, take part in the initiative's activity and expand it together with the family — from the page to the path.

The partnership model

Three tiers of involvement

The Bishvileinu nonprofit works to raise additional resources to expand the initiative and subsidize the delegations coming to Israel.

BASIC

Local guidance

Financial support for all stages of local implementation: carrying out a local memorial project, organizing representation from the bereaved family, and spreading the fallen soldier's story.

PREMIUM

Partnership in Israel

+ Everything in BASIC

In addition — involvement in the memorial project in Israel, financial partnership and involvement in its growth and dissemination.

PLATINUM

Leadership and expansion

+ Everything in PREMIUM

In addition — delegations to Israel (subsidized by the nonprofit), significant promotion of the initiative and its replication in further places.
