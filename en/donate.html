Where the money goes

Every shekel is devoted to the path — to connection, guidance, and widening the circle of memory. Here's where.

🤝

Subsidizing encounters

Funding memorial encounters between bereaved families and communities — from the first meeting to partnership.

📚

Educational materials

Developing and distributing educational kits about the fallen, tailored to each community.

✈️

Subsidizing delegations

Bringing community delegations to Israel, for a visit and active partnership in the memorial initiatives.
