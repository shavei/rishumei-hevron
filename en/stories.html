Stories

The living wall of memory

Behind every memorial initiative stands a person, a story and a set of values. Here the stories of the fallen and the initiatives founded in their memory will unfold.

What will be revealed here

From a story — to a path

Every story is a gateway. We gather the stories of the fallen and the living memorial initiatives that grew from them — agriculture, education, settlement, welfare and health — so that communities around the world can get to know them, adopt them and carry them on.

Coming soon

The archive is being built now

We are carefully gathering the stories and the initiatives, together with the families. Soon a full archive will be revealed here — filtered by field — from which every community can choose the initiative closest to its heart.
