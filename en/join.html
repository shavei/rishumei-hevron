Join the circle

Let's reach out, and join in

Let's widen the circle of action — for them and for us. Find your way to join.

Who takes part

Two sides, one path

🕍 The partner side — communities & organizations

Jewish schools Synagogues Jewish federations Charity & welfare organizations Youth movements

🌱 The giving side — memorial initiatives in Israel

Initiatives founded by bereaved families in memory of the fallen — with an emphasis on active projects that strengthen Israeli society.

Coming soon: individuals and families will also be able to join.

Choose your path

Which audience do you belong to?

Track A

🕍 Jewish communities & organizations

You're a school, synagogue, federation or Jewish youth organization that wants to join the path, adopt a memorial initiative and become an active partner in building Israel — while strengthening Jewish identity and resilience in your community.

Track B

🌱 Bereaved families & memorial initiatives

Have you founded an initiative in memory of your loved one and want to widen the circle of remembrance? We'll introduce your initiative to Jewish communities around the world, match you with a community that will adopt and grow your dream — and accompany you every step of the way.

Track C

🤲 Donors & strategic partners

Want to support expanding the initiative to more communities? Your donation subsidizes visits, funds educational materials and enables more families and more communities to meet one another. Every shekel goes directly to the path.

Levels of involvement

Choose your level of partnership

See the full breakdown on the "How It Works" page — BASIC, PREMIUM and PLATINUM, with an explanation of what each track includes.

BASIC

Local guidance

Support through every stage of local implementation: a memorial project, family representation, and sharing the story.

PREMIUM

Partnership in Israel

Everything in BASIC, plus — direct involvement in the memorial project in Israel and financial partnership.

PLATINUM

Leadership & expansion

Everything in PREMIUM, plus — subsidized delegations to Israel and replicating the initiative in additional places.
